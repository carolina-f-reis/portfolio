Hi my name is Carolina and I’m a Digital Experience Designer based in Lisbon.

Thinking and crafting digital experiences with a multidisciplinary background and skillset.

About me

I’m passionate about usability and always strive to create interfaces that balance beauty and efficiency.

I studied Audiovisual and Multimedia at the School of Communication and Media Studies from the Lisbon Polytechnic, where I received my Bachelor’s degree in 2020.

Because of my interest in behavioral psychology, digital products and user experience, I decided to readjust my focus and I’m currently taking a Postgraduate course in Digital Experience Design.

Besides design I love to travel, to play guitar and to annoy my two cats from time to time.

Work

GreenShare

Mobile App

GreenShare is a social app created from one simple question - how can we improve health in everyday places? The app provides search options of nearby community vegetable gardens and users can place their order in a secure and easy way.

Nature Calls

Mobile App

Nature Calls is an app that allows you to search for the nearest toilets, being possible to define certain parameters such as distance and some other functions such as wheelchair access. Besides, it allows its users to give ratings and reviews.

FBAUL's website redesign

Website

This project was a redesign of a faculty website. The main aim of this redesign was to develop a proposal to improve the website experience with a focus on the segment of candidates. Lasted 5 weeks and was made by a team of 4 people.
